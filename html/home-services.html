A different kind of consulting company

About us

A3 Advisory Services is a small woman owned business that provides management consulting and technology training to federal and commercial clients. We believe that the hardest aspect of building a great company is finding and nurturing the best employees, and providing those amazing people to clients’ at rates that are competitive. Our value is in delivering outstanding people at affordable prices, and using a low overhead structure to develop a familial feeling within our company.

Our services are constantly changing as we add new people to our team. While we are primarily a management and information technology consultancy, we know that services will grow with each new hire. We will work to amend this section to include newly offered capabilities.

Our growth strategy

Our growth strategy is really simple. Be awesome. By demonstrating our loyalty and dedication to our clients and our willingness to put our clients’ needs ahead of corporate needs (see Culture & Values), we believe that we will attract the kind of employees we want, and the kinds of clients we want.

Our goal is not to become the next large consultancy. Sure, we want sustainable long term growth, but it needs to be bounded to maintain the quality of our culture, and the quality of our staff. We will not become a “butts in seats” beltway bandit contractor. We want more, and we want people who want more from their company.

We want employee referrals to be the leading cause of hiring within our company. We want client referrals and requests to drive our contract growth.

Locations and offices

We are primarily located in Northern Virginia. Because most of our employees consult directly for federal clients in their spaces, we have opted to maintain a lower overhead cost structure to enable diverting more of its resources to our employees.

This has two effects – it enables us to retain highly skilled and valuable employees while paying them competitively high compensation and keeping a rate structure that is competitive in the current marketplace.

Our Values

  1. The Client’s Needs are More Important than the Company’s
    You want to make a difference for your client. If you didn’t, you wouldn’t be looking at this company. We firmly believe that in the long run, putting our clients’ interests first is in our best interest. Whenever the two are in conflict, the clients’ interests win.
  2. Amazing Talent Should Be Recognized
    We value contribution over title, and impact over talk. We are an action oriented company, a group of “doers” who act first. Sure, sometimes that means we make mistakes. But we prefer mistakes to inaction. We trust the people we hire, and we want you to make things happen. We’ll provide you as much as you need, and help if you ask. But we won’t be mad that you went off and tried to make some awesome happen on your own.
  3. The Company Should Stay Out of Your Way
    We believe that the best employees don’t need management. They need to be put into a position where they can make a difference, and set free to make magic happen. We pride ourselves on bringing together talented teams of amazing people. Once that is done, our job is to get out of the way.
  4. It’s Boring When You’re Not Growing
    We know that your growth is important to retention, happiness, and ultimately customer satisfaction! We want you to continue to seek knowledge and skills that align with our opportunities and our client’s needs. We support professional development through training, conferences, and tuition assistance.
  5. You Should Get Credit for Bringing Home the Bacon
    Our policy hasn’t been fully documented, but a key aspect of our value system is recognizing the contributions of our team. If you’re responsible for bringing on a new client, adding new awesome staff, or otherwise responsible for generating new profits for the company, you deserve a piece of that pie. You know you think so, but here at A3, we think so too. We also know that working at a small company is different from working at a large one, sometimes in scary ways. There is less “definition” around hierarchy and level within the organization. While this sometimes might feel like there are less avenues for advancement, the opposite is actually true. Our flat organization gives you the ability to get directly involved in growing the business, and reaping the rewards that follow!
  6. Creating Drama is an Antipattern
    We are bringing together an insanely competent group of people. People who don't need step by step guidance. We believe that these kinds of people hate drama - especially the imaginary and created kinds that exist in large companies. Office politics will not be tolerated. It is company policy to use imagination to solve customer problems - not to create drama in the office.
  7. You Only Live Once, so Let’s Have Some Fun Together
    We don't force our employees to attend company events, but we do believe that you will like the people you work with, and will want to bring your family into the fold. We sponsor events and activities to bring our team closer together and to share experiences, skills, and friendship.

If you want to hire great people, you need to have great benefits.
